About Aryan Arya Associates

Aryan Arya Associates Ltd is a collective of farmers, traders, and agricultural experts working to transform traditional agricultural sourcing into a transparent and traceable supply chain.

We integrate modern technology, farmer partnerships, and strict quality control to deliver export-grade garlic and dried fruits.

Our mission is to ensure food safety, quality, and sustainability while building reliable farm-to-factory supply systems.

Field to Factory Process

  1. Step 1: Farm Sourcing

    We work directly with farmers and monitor production.

  2. Step 2: Processing and Drying

    Products undergo controlled drying and handling.

  3. Step 3: Quality Control

    Each batch is inspected and digitally tracked.

  4. Step 4: Packaging and Distribution

    Products are hygienically packed and delivered.

Fully Traceable Supply Chain

Our digital traceability system allows every batch to be tracked from farm to delivery. We monitor:

  • Farm origin
  • Harvest data
  • Processing
  • Quality inspection
  • Shipment tracking

This ensures transparency and buyer confidence.
